About AMH Civil

One Accountable Team

AMH Civil plans and delivers complex and brownfield civil infrastructure across Victoria — with in-house engineers, an experienced team of supervisory staff, a directly employed workforce, and a modern fleet of plant and equipment.

Our in-house engineering team provides value engineering, design solutions, shop drawing development and 3D modelling before mobilisation and during delivery — building in upfront value that reduces risk for our clients.
