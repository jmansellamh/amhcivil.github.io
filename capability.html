What We Deliver

Capability

A directly employed, engineering-led team with the systems, plant, and experience to manage complex civil works from programme through to handover.

Lower Risk

One Accountable Team

Our supervisors and crews have worked together across years of projects and under various client network authority systems and access requirements. One accountable team means fewer interfaces, faster decisions, and no finger-pointing.

Greater Value

In-House Engineering

A chartered and registered team of civil, geotechnical and geospatial engineering resources to provide value engineering, design solutions, shop drawing development and 3D modelling before mobilisation and during delivery.

Increased Certainty

Integrated Management

An Integrated Management System built around our operations by internal staff, certified to ISO 9001, ISO 14001, and ISO 45001 and accredited under the Office of the Federal Safety Commissioner's WHS Scheme.

01

Concrete Structures

Coordinated in-house trades for civil preparation, steel fixing, formworking and concreting activities — from footings and slabs through to complex structural works.

  • Management of set out plans and temporary works designs
  • Structural concrete capacity including falsework and access systems
  • Bridge construction and composite steel-concrete structures
  • Tilt-up panels and precast coordination
  • Shop drawing review and 3D clash detection

02

Pavements

R2 prequalified for road construction on the DTP Victoria road network, with internal grading plant, kerb and channel resources, and total station technology to guarantee ride quality conformance.

  • R2 prequalification for DTP road network works
  • Development of surface models and coordination of design set outs
  • Value engineering for pavement composition and geotechnical review
  • Internal grading plant and kerb and channel resources
  • Permits, inspections and hold points managed by AMH Civil engineers
  • Existing relationships with STS prequalified traffic signal contractors

03

Buildings & Process Infrastructure

Registered with the Building and Plumbing Commission for low rise commercial construction, with integrated civil and utility installation capability and a directly employed workforce to de-risk build costs.

  • BPC registered for low rise commercial construction
  • Brownfield planning and delivery including refurbishment and extensions
  • Integrated civil and utility installation with direct workforce
  • Management of subcontractors and supervision of high risk work
  • Management systems appropriate for food-grade and regulated environments

04

Stormwater & Utilities

Specialist personnel and equipment for deep trenching in tight corridors, with engineering office staff to support clash detection and route planning prior to mobilisation to mitigate downtime.

  • Safe work systems for deep excavations and large diameter installation
  • VEDN accreditation and ESI trained personnel for power authority networks
  • Trained plumbing resources for connections and minor water mains
  • 3D modelling for clash detection and conduit route planning
  • Bypass pumping and live network connections

05

Regional & Remote Civil

Regional based supervisors and operators to service works across Victoria, with planning and project management staff to administrate permits and logistics for remote serviced projects.

  • Regional supervisors and operators based across Victoria
  • Key accounts with quarries and regional partners
  • Servicing projects from Mildura to Mallacoota
  • Permit management and logistics for remote projects
  • Self-sufficient mobilisation including accommodation and site establishment
