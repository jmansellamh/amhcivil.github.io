Transformer Bay Slabs & Switch Room Footings

Following building permit delays and some design changes, AMH Civil accelerated the construction of transformer bay slabs to ensure set-downs were complete ready for scheduled delivery.

Complex Deep Conduits

AMH Civil modelled conduit runs and connections in 3D with sewer and stormwater, to perform clash detection and confirm bend radii and locations prior to construction. This allowed for safe installation concurrent with transformer bay footings and mitigated delays.

Tilt-Up Panels

AMH Civil reviewed shop drawings against design drawings, and worked with Samco Precast for the timely fabrication and seamless installation of transformer bay tilt-up panels on site.
